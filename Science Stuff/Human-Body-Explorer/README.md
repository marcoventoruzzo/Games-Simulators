# Inside You! — Human Body Explorer

An interactive, child-friendly anatomy explorer built with Python and Streamlit.

Children can switch between a girl and boy model, peel through four anatomical layers, isolate major organ systems, inspect organs by hovering or tapping, and play a short “Find the organ” challenge.

The drawings are simplified teaching diagrams. They are not diagnostic or perfectly to scale.
